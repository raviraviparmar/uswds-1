Mission

To protect life, property, and the environment through prevention, education, and emergency response.

We serve our community with professionalism, compassion, and dedication — always ready to respond when needed most.

Vision

To be a leader in fire and emergency services — recognized for our commitment to innovation, safety, and community partnership.

We envision a safer community built on prevention, preparedness, and trust.

Values

Our values define who we are and guide every action we take as public servants.

  • Courage – Act with bravery and strength in the face of danger.
  • Integrity – Always do what’s right, even when no one is watching.
  • Service – Committed to protecting and helping our community.
  • Teamwork – Work together to achieve our mission.
  • Compassion – Care for those we serve and our fellow responders
