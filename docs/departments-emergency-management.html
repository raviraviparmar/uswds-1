Mission

To enhance community resilience by leading emergency preparedness, coordinating response operations, and supporting recovery efforts to protect lives, property, and critical infrastructure.

Vision

A disaster-ready community built on awareness, collaboration, and a unified approach to emergency management.

Values

Preparedness

We empower our community to anticipate and withstand emergencies.

Reliability

We respond quickly and effectively when crises occur.

Resilience

We support long-term recovery and strengthen our community’s ability to bounce back.

Coordination

We work seamlessly with local, state, and federal partners.
