Downtown Smart Lighting Upgrade

Open
Public Works
Posted: 12/15/2025

Description

The City of Riverview is seeking proposals to replace 450 existing high-pressure sodium streetlights with smart-controlled LED fixtures. This includes the installation of a central management system (CMS) and a 3-year maintenance agreement.

Q1: Can the response deadline be extended if a future addendum contains major technical changes?

Answer: Per city procurement policy, if an addendum significantly alters the scope of work within 72 hours of the closing date, the deadline will be extended by a minimum of five (5) business days to allow all vendors sufficient time to adjust their proposals.

Posted: December 26, 2025

Q2: Does the City require the use of a specific subcontractor for the disposal of the old high-pressure sodium fixtures?

Answer: No. However, the contractor must provide documentation proving that all hazardous components (such as mercury-containing bulbs) are disposed of or recycled at an EPA-certified facility in accordance with state environmental laws.

Posted: December 30, 2025

Q3: Is the 5% Bid Bond required to be a physical document, or can a digital surety bond be uploaded?

Answer: Digital surety bonds are accepted and preferred. If using a digital bond, please ensure the verification link or code is clearly visible on the uploaded PDF document. Physical checks must be received by the Procurement Office before the bid closing time.

Posted: January 02, 2026
